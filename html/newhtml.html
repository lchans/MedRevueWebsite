Teams

Students across all faculties are welcome in every aspect of Med Revue. You don’t even need to know anything; as long as you’re keen, we’ll teach you! You can get involved in many aspects within the production. There’s an interest area, skill set and time commitment that fits everyone! If you’re unsure, send us an email. Do note that due to time commitment, some teams are exclusive to others. Click on a button below to find out more about a team you’re interested in!



What do I do in cast?
Cast are our actors in the show! They learn lines, sing numbers, dance jigs and bring the content the Society writes to life!
What skills do I learn in Cast?
  • Learn how to embody a character (often in caricature) and project it on a stage
  • Stage presence, articulation and vocal projection
  • Sing in a choral number
  • Dance under crazy lights and smoke
    • What is the time commitment in Cast?
    Approximately 15 hours a week (and more as show approaches), Cast is a big commitment. How this does include down time and a lot of socialising! Our Producers and other teams also make sure you are looked after and dont crumble into an exhausted mess.
    What do I need to do to get into Cast?
    No auditions or experience necessary! Cast selection is based on enthusiasm, attitude and availability. The Directors will be holding introductory rehearsals for you to try out Cast before committing.
